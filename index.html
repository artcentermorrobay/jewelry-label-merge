Paste Data

Paste tab-separated or comma-separated rows copied from a spreadsheet. The app auto-detects whether your data has 5 columns (Name, Title, Medium, Price, Number) or 7 columns (First, Last, Name, Title, Medium, Price, Number). A header row is auto-detected and skipped. Leave cells empty where not needed — for example, leave First/Last blank if Name is already combined.
